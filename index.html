My Favourite Types of Weather

By: Skyler :D

Rank Weather Type
1 Stormy
2 Overcast
3 Windy

Stormy

Stormy weathers are my favourite because it makes it feel like the world is ending and I personally cannot wait for the world to come to an end.

Other reasons why storms are great:

  • cool air!
  • lightning!
  • rain sounds!
  • the refreshing smell of rain!
  • thunder!

In conclusion, stormy weather is very cool.

Overcast

I like it when it is overcast as it means there is both:

  1. no sun and
  2. no rain,

so it won't be so hot that we feel like we're melting, and we won't have to deal with the inconvenience of finding an umbrella.

Overcast weather also provides a very good environment to fall asleep in, especially during early mornings when you have a lesson you don't really want to attend.

I just really love the feeling where it's just about to rain, but it's not quite there yet.


Windy

Windy weather is very relaxing and cooling.

Having the wind blowing gently always makes me feel like I can close my eyes and melt away. Often, it has a very cleansing effect.

That is, unless the wind is blowing very fast and you feel like your face will be whipped off by the wind.

Excluding that one circumstance, windy weather is always a joy to have.
